I am a writer, researcher, and organiser working across contemporary art, publishing, commoning, and collective study.

This website gathers selected projects, writing, research strands, and leftover fragments from ongoing work.

Practice

My practice moves between writing, collaborative publishing, reading groups, workshops, and informal infrastructures for cultural work.

I am interested in how people gather around texts, how knowledge is shared, and how independent cultural practices can sustain themselves without simply reproducing institutional or market logics.
